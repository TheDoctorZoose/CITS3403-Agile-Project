About

This app allows for tracking, analysis and sharing of your board game data!

Every time you have a board game session, simply search for the name of the board game and enter the details of your last games through our interface, or instead batch import a CSV or JSON file containing your previous board gaming history.

Your play data is automatically analysed and summaries and charts are displayed so you can see insights on your play history!
These analyses can be shared with other users on the platform so you can compare your play statistics with others you know!
